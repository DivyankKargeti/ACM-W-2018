About Us

Who are we?

UPES ACM-W STUDENT CHAPTER

The UPES ACM-W was found in April, 2015 with an aim to realize the dynamic image of women in the field of computers and information technology and to provide them with a platform to showcase their technical skills. We at UPES ACM-W try to follow in their steps to create a community where all are given equal opportunities and everyone works together in harmony. UPES ACM-W Student Chapter is reserved exclusively for women with an aim to highlight the fact that women are as competent as men in the field of technology. To achieve this, we organize various activities and events to bring out the programmer and genius within each and every individual. We strive to create a healthy environment for women that encourages them to step forward and showcase their talent.

SPY-C

The C language provides you the basis of building your interest in programming. Spy-C provides you the exact guidance for getting the language at your fingertips. The senior members help the budding computer science engineers to get a hands-on learning with the language.

Workshops


Helping to establish and bloom the various interests of our members, Android App Development, Web Designing and Development, and VFX Workshop are some of the most dedicated and elaborate workshops to get the basics and fundamentals right.

CODEANYTIME


It was initiated to create an environment to code at our university. Everyone utilizes the time they have to produce what they can with no boundaries.The initiative was taken by UPES ACM and UPES ACM-W Student Chapters.

CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY (CSR)


The chapter takes a moral stance for cause, enabling the underprivileged by giving them the opportunity to learn and grow.
